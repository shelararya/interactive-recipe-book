Cheesy Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 veggie patties
  • 2 cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup
  • Butter for toasting

Steps

  1. Toast buns with butter.
  2. Cook patties until crisp.
  3. Place lettuce, tomato, onion on bun.
  4. Add patty and cheese slice.
  5. Top with sauces and serve hot.
