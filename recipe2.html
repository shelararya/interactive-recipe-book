Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté.
  3. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add garam masala and salt.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 mins.
  6. Add cream, simmer for 2 mins, and serve hot.
