French Fries

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Cold water

Steps to Make

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin sticks.
  2. Soak them in cold water for 30 minutes.
  3. Drain and pat dry with a towel.
  4. Heat oil and fry the potatoes until golden and crispy.
  5. Drain excess oil and sprinkle with salt. Serve hot.
