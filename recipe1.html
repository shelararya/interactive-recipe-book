Creamy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps to Make

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic.
  3. Add heavy cream and simmer.
  4. Mix in parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Add pasta and toss to coat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.
