Veg Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves)

Steps

  1. Cook rice 70% and set aside.
  2. Sauté whole spices and onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, vegetables, and curd.
  4. Add biryani masala, salt, and cook well.
  5. Layer rice and veggie masala, cover and cook.
  6. Serve hot with raita.
