Momos

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water to knead dough

Steps to Make

  1. Knead the flour with water and a pinch of salt to make soft dough.
  2. Mix cabbage, onion, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper for filling.
  3. Roll small circles of dough, place filling, and fold into momos shape.
  4. Steam the momos for 10–12 minutes in a steamer.
  5. Serve hot with red chutney or dip.
