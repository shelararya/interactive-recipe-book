Tadka Maggi

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Oil and water

Steps

  1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and let splutter.
  2. Add onion, chili, and tomato and cook.
  3. Add turmeric and Maggi masala.
  4. Add noodles and water, cook until done.
  5. Serve hot with coriander garnish.
