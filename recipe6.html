Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Veg toppings (onion, capsicum, corn)
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Oregano and olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Spread sauce over pizza base.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts.
